Footprint

2021-03-01

It was the heaviest snow this year. I went out when it was dark outside. Snow fell on my hands, and then it became water. There were many footprints on the ground. The whole world was all quiet.

Suddenly, the sun rose up. Everything began to wake up. It became brighter and brighter now. The snow was melting. The footprints faded.

I wanted to leave some footprints on the ground! I wanted everything in the world, such as flowers, grasses, to hear my voice!

Let the world hear my voice…
